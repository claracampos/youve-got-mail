About The Movie

You've Got Mail is a 1998 romantic comedy film directed by Nora Ephron. It tells the story of two business rivals, Kathleen Kelly (Meg Ryan) and Joe Fox (Tom Hanks), who fall in love with each other after meeting in an online chatroom.

About The App

This web-application was developed and styled by:

Clara Campos

Prince Edward Island, Canada

GitHub | Blog

This web-app was built on Node.js, Socket.io, a healthy dose of nostalgia and the developer’s genuine love for Nora Ephron movies starring Meg Ryan. (If you haven’t seen When Harry Met Sally yet, you absolutely should.)

The chat rooms in this app are named after places in You've Got Mail:

  • New York City: where the film is set.
  • Shop Around The Corner: Kathleen's bookstore.
  • Fox Books: a chain of mega-bookstores run by Joe and his family.
  • Over 30: the chatroom where Joe and Kathleen meet.
